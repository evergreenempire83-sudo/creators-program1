Our Story

Founded in June 2025, EverGreen Empire emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to create sustainable income streams for content creators in the digital age. We recognized that while creators were building massive audiences, they were missing out on significant revenue opportunities from the music used in their content.

Today, we stand as the world's preeminent entertainment company, working across every aspect of modern entertainment. Our unique model bridges the gap between music rights holders and content creators, ensuring everyone benefits from the content ecosystem.

We work with recording artists and producers, songwriters and more, supporting our diverse roster of talent via artist management, music publishing, touring, production, and strategic brand development.
