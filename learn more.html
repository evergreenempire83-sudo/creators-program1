Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I see my first royalty payment? +

Your first payment will arrive within 90 days of your first eligible content. This allows us to collect royalties from all platforms worldwide. After your first payment, you'll receive monthly payments.

Why does the audio need to be at least 15% volume? +

Content ID systems across platforms require the music to be clearly audible to properly detect and attribute royalties. Audio below 15% may not trigger detection, meaning you'd miss out on earnings.

Can I use EverGreen sounds on multiple platforms? +

Yes! We encourage cross-posting to YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels, and other platforms. Each platform generates separate royalty streams, maximizing your overall earnings.

What happens if my video is shorter than 35 seconds? +

Videos shorter than 35 seconds may not generate significant royalties due to platform limitations and content ID thresholds. For optimal earnings, aim for 1+ minute content whenever possible.
